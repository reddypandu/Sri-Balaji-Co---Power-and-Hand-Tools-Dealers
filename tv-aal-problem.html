TV Automatic Audio Level (AAL) Repair

Is your TV sound increasing or decreasing on its own? We specialize in repairing AAL (Automatic Audio Level) issues in LED, LCD, Plasma, and Smart TVs, ensuring smooth and stable sound performance every time you watch.

From sudden sound jumps, inconsistent volume between channels, to AAL settings not functioning properly — our skilled technicians provide accurate diagnosis and lasting repair solutions. Using advanced tools and genuine spare parts, we restore your TV’s sound to its original clarity and balance.

If your TV audio keeps fluctuating or the AAL option fails to respond, we deliver effective solutions to stabilize the sound.

Our service includes doorstep visits, transparent pricing, and warranty-backed repairs to ensure long-term performance.
